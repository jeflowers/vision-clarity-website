Frequently Asked Questions

Am I a good candidate for LASIK?

Good candidates for LASIK are typically 18 years or older with stable vision for at least one year. A comprehensive eye exam during your consultation will determine if LASIK is right for you based on your eye health, prescription, and corneal thickness.

Is LASIK painful?

LASIK is generally not painful. We use numbing eye drops before the procedure, and patients typically feel only slight pressure during the brief surgery. Some mild discomfort, irritation, or dryness may occur during the first 24-48 hours of recovery.

How long does recovery take?

Most patients notice improved vision immediately after surgery, with vision continuing to stabilize over the next few days. Many return to work within 1-2 days. Complete healing may take 3-6 months, though most visual stabilization occurs within the first few weeks.

How much does LASIK cost?

The cost of LASIK at Vision Clarity Institute ranges from $1,800 to $2,500 per eye, depending on your specific vision needs and the technology selected. We offer flexible financing options with affordable monthly payments.
