Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for my consultation?

Please bring your current glasses or contact lens prescription, a list of medications you're taking, and any relevant medical history. If you wear contact lenses, please discontinue wear for at least 3 days before your consultation for soft contacts, or 2-3 weeks for hard or rigid gas permeable contacts.

How long does a consultation appointment take?

Initial consultations typically take 90 minutes. During this time, we'll perform comprehensive diagnostic tests, discuss your vision goals, review your options, and answer all your questions.

Is there a fee for the consultation?

No, Vision Clarity Institute offers complimentary consultations to determine if you're a candidate for laser vision correction. If additional specialized testing is required, your consultant will discuss any potential costs before proceeding.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financing companies to offer flexible payment plans with 0% interest options. Our team will discuss these options during your consultation.
