Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during a free consultation?

During your free consultation, we'll perform a comprehensive eye exam, discuss your vision goals, and determine if you're a candidate for LASIK or another vision correction procedure. You'll have the opportunity to ask questions and learn about your options.

How do I prepare for my consultation?

Please bring your glasses or contact lens prescription, a list of medications you're taking, and your vision insurance information if applicable. If you wear contact lenses, please stop wearing them for at least 3 days (soft lenses) or 2 weeks (rigid lenses) before your appointment.

Is financing available for LASIK surgery?

Yes, we offer several financing options through trusted healthcare financing partners. During your consultation, our patient coordinator will explain the available plans, including 0% interest options for qualified patients.

How soon can I schedule my LASIK procedure after consultation?

If you're a suitable candidate for LASIK, you can typically schedule your procedure within 1-2 weeks of your consultation, depending on availability. Some patients choose to schedule their procedure the same day as their consultation.
