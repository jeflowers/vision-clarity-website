Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the form on this page, calling our office at (800) 555-2020, or using our online booking system. Our staff will help you find a convenient time for your initial evaluation.

Please bring your current glasses or contact lens prescription, a list of medications you're taking, your insurance card, and any medical records related to your eyes. If you wear contact lenses, please remove them 48 hours prior to your appointment for soft contacts, or 3 weeks prior for rigid gas permeable lenses.

We accept most major insurance plans and flexible spending accounts (FSAs). While most insurance plans don't cover LASIK or other refractive surgeries, they may cover the initial consultation. Our staff will help determine your coverage and discuss financing options.

Your initial consultation will take approximately 90 minutes. This includes a comprehensive eye exam, discussion of your vision correction options, and time to ask questions. Our goal is to provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Yes, we offer a variety of financing options including interest-free payment plans and healthcare credit options. We work with several healthcare financing companies to help make your vision correction procedure affordable.
