Our Services

Advanced vision correction solutions tailored to your needs

Traditional LASIK

Our standard LASIK procedure uses a microkeratome blade to create a thin flap in the cornea. This established technique offers excellent vision correction for nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism with a quick recovery time.

  • Quick 15-minute procedure
  • Minimal discomfort
  • 24-48 hour recovery
  • Established safety record
Schedule a Consultation

Custom Bladeless LASIK

Our premium all-laser LASIK procedure creates a corneal flap using a femtosecond laser instead of a blade. This advanced technique allows for more precise corneal mapping and customized treatment, resulting in better outcomes for patients with complex prescriptions.

  • Bladeless technology
  • Customized corneal mapping
  • Higher precision for complex cases
  • Reduced risk of complications
Schedule a Consultation

PRK (Photorefractive Keratectomy)

PRK is an excellent alternative for patients who aren't candidates for LASIK. Instead of creating a flap, PRK removes the outer layer of the cornea before reshaping, making it suitable for patients with thin corneas or certain corneal abnormalities.

  • Ideal for thin corneas
  • No flap-related complications
  • Excellent long-term stability
  • 3-5 day initial recovery
Schedule a Consultation

Presbyopia Correction

We offer specialized procedures to address age-related near vision loss (presbyopia). These treatments can reduce or eliminate dependence on reading glasses through monovision LASIK or advanced lens-based solutions.

  • Freedom from reading glasses
  • Monovision options
  • Customized to your lifestyle
  • Multifocal capabilities
