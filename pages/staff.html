Charles Wesley Flowers, MD, FACS

OVERVIEW

Charles W. Flowers Jr., MD, received his undergraduate degree in Chemistry from Stanford University in 1985 and his medical degree from Cornell University Medical College in 1989.

Following his ophthalmology residency training at the Charles R. Drew University of Medicine in Los Angeles, California, he completed a cornea and refractive surgery fellowship, earning the Award of Excellence in Resident Training.

Recognized as one of the nation's top eye surgeons, Dr. Flowers is dedicated to advancing teleophthalmology, diabetic retinopathy screening, and visual enhancement in sports.
