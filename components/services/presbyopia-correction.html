Presbyopia Correction

Presbyopia is an age-related condition that affects nearly everyone over 40, making it difficult to focus on close objects. At Vision Clarity Institute, we offer several solutions to address presbyopia.

Our Presbyopia Correction Options

Monovision LASIK

In this approach, one eye is corrected for distance vision and the other for near vision. Your brain learns to use the appropriate eye depending on what you're looking at. This can reduce or eliminate the need for reading glasses.

Multifocal LASIK

Using advanced laser technology, we can create multiple focus zones on your cornea, allowing clear vision at various distances. This advanced technique can provide excellent results for appropriate candidates.

Refractive Lens Exchange (RLE)

For some patients, especially those over 50, replacing the eye's natural lens with an advanced multifocal lens implant may be the best option. This procedure is similar to cataract surgery but is performed before cataracts develop.

Determining the Right Option for You

During your consultation, our specialists will evaluate your vision, discuss your lifestyle and visual needs, and recommend the most suitable presbyopia correction approach for you.
