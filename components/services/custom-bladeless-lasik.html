Custom Bladeless LASIK

Custom Bladeless LASIK is our premium, all-laser LASIK procedure that offers enhanced precision and personalization for optimal visual outcomes.

How It Works

In Custom Bladeless LASIK, we use two different lasers: a femtosecond laser to create the corneal flap (instead of a blade), and an advanced wavefront-guided excimer laser for corneal reshaping. This all-laser approach allows for more precise flap creation and customized treatment based on your unique corneal map.

Key Benefits

  • Greater precision in flap creation
  • Personalized treatment plan based on unique eye characteristics
  • Improved night vision and reduced glare compared to traditional LASIK
  • Potential to correct higher-order aberrations (visual distortions)
  • Excellent option for patients with thin corneas or higher prescriptions

Is Custom Bladeless LASIK Right for You?

While slightly more expensive than traditional LASIK, Custom Bladeless LASIK may be worth considering if you have complex vision issues, high-order aberrations, or want the most advanced technology available. Our surgeons will assess your specific needs during your consultation.
